Ideation II

March 27
2014

General idea seems to be a top down, almost map-like area where the main "explorer" walks around for supplies/food/enemies.
Possibilities for top down:
-new area where you have to navigate a randomly generated maze
-new area randomly generated with monsters
Have a main "family" you need to protect/feed/shelter. The main character (you) has to get the supplies necessary.
Random outcomes from different events, resulting in either profit or damage to your team/character.
